Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets through dream-sharing technology. He's offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can plant an idea into a target's subconscious — a process known as "inception."
The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
